## How Fractions Get Encoded

- **Dominant community** = main lighting, color temperature, and mascot energy (e.g., 45% Jhana = moonlight-violet base + lotus serpent vibe)
- **Secondary communities** = subtle accents the mascot is "holding" or "wearing" (Laughing Bodhisattva holding a tiny prism shard = 30% Qualia; faint recursive glitches in the flames = 15% LLM Whisperers)
- **Tertiary** = background texture or border motif only

The viewer just feels "this card has contemplative depth with a tech-weird edge" — it's intuitive and mythic. No bars, no dots, no percentages on the card itself.
