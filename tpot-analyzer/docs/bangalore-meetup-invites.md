**Methods used (11 approaches across 2 rounds):**

- `T` — Tweet content (BLR neighborhoods, local companies, civic markers)
- `P` — Topical relevance (PKM/AI/exocortex in bio OR tweet text, 312 users)

- `Reply` — Reply graph to confirmed BLR accounts (21 accounts ≥2 BLR replies)
- `Comm` — Community membership clustering (dead end — ID format mismatch)
- `Follower` — User's own followers × BLR (531 followers, 1 BLR match)
- `Peak` — @peakbengaluru engagement signal (10 likers, 3 tweeters)
